## Overview

diegomezzo.com is my personal portfolio and digital identity website.

The project was built to present who I am as a Software Engineer, founder and builder through a premium static web experience focused on clarity, trust, performance and visual impact.

The website showcases my professional background, engineering experience, founder journey, digital products and contact channels through a refined dark visual identity, responsive layouts, optimized assets and motion-driven interactions.

Rather than being a simple online resume, diegomezzo.com was designed as a personal brand platform: a place where technical credibility, product vision and professional identity come together in one polished digital experience.

It represents both my current professional path and the direction I am building toward: creating software products, digital experiences and business solutions with strong engineering foundations.

### Website Architecture

diegomezzo.com follows a static website architecture focused on speed, simplicity and control.

The website is structured around a handcrafted frontend experience where layout, visual identity, responsive behavior and motion are managed directly through HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

The project does not rely on a CMS or heavy frontend framework. This approach keeps the website lightweight, fast and fully tailored to the intended personal brand experience.

The architecture is designed around:

* Static page rendering
* Responsive layout systems
* Optimized media delivery
* Motion-enhanced sections
* SEO and social metadata
* Clean deployment workflow
* Strong visual consistency across devices
